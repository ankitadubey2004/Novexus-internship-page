Internship Roles

Software Engineering Intern

Build. Test. Learn. Repeat. Join our engineering team to work on real code that ships. You'll collaborate on backend systems, APIs, and scalable services using modern technologies. If you're passionate about problem-solving and clean code, this is your playground.

Know more

UI/UX Design Intern

Create intuitive, beautiful experiences that users love. Work alongside our design team to research, prototype, and craft interfaces for our products. You'll gain hands-on experience with design systems, user testing, and the full design process.

Know more

Data Science Intern

Turn data into insights and insights into action. Work with our data team to analyze complex datasets, build predictive models, and create visualizations that drive business decisions. Perfect for analytical minds who love solving puzzles with data.

Know more

Product Management Intern

Bridge the gap between users, business, and technology. Help define product roadmaps, gather requirements, and coordinate with engineering and design teams. You'll learn how to prioritize features and make data-driven product decisions.

Build Your Future with Us

Real Projects, Real Impact

No busywork here. Interns and early career hires work on meaningful projects that go live — contributing directly to Novexus products and platforms. Work on live projects that directly contribute to Novexus products. You'll write code, solve real problems, and see your work in action — not in a sandbox.

Continuous Learning

Attend weekly tech talks, workshops, and mentorship sessions. We'll support you in picking up new tools, frameworks, and ways of thinking. Access to workshops, tech talks, certification opportunities, and curated learning paths — whether you're into cloud, AI, product, or design.

Mentorship

Each intern is paired with a mentor who provides regular guidance, feedback, and support to accelerate your learning and confidence. You'll be paired with experienced mentors who offer regular check-ins, code reviews, career advice, and growth support.

Cross-Team Collaboration

Get hands-on experience working across departments — engineering, design, product, and more — just like a full-time team member. You'll work cross-functionally with engineers, designers, PMs, and marketers. Our open culture means your ideas get heard — and sometimes even implemented.

Remote-Friendly Flexibility

We offer flexible working hours and a hybrid-friendly setup so you can work in the way that suits you best. Work from our modern office or remotely — or blend both. We care more about outcomes than locations.

Fun & Community

Team lunches, virtual hangouts, game nights, and intern-exclusive events. We work hard but make time to celebrate together. Virtual games, team lunches, and intern meetups keep things fun. We celebrate progress, not just milestones.
